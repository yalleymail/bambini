Learn About StarKid

In recent times on some media discussions, there has been heightened worry of Ghanaian kids ‘being lost’. Most parents no longer take their kids to their hometowns or even speak the local dialect with them. It looks like in the nearest future, our heritage would have been forgotten and another man’s culture embraced. Fast foods are soon replacing our typical local food and amazingly some kids have no idea of some local meals. Our tradition teaches children to be respectful and they grow thereby.Meet Bambini Show Presenters as they brings you nothing but the best education, entertaining and inspiring contents to your doorstep.

The program is timely in helping shape our kids into responsible adults. This show is therefore created to arouse interest in kids as they watch their peers perform. Partnering with the show is a course in the right direction. We are hopeful we will salvage our heritage through the reality show ‘’STAR KID AFRICA’’.

you can watch Bambini Show on Saturdays on GHOne at 8:30am, Kantanka TV at 9am, TV Africa at 3pm, Channel 247 at 4pm and sundays at 3pm on GN TV Junior. Bambini show is your number 1 family tv show.
